About Me

Dr. Grace, PhD

I am a passionate geo-researcher with over 15 years of experience in field exploration and laboratory analysis. My research focuses on understanding Earth's geological history and its implications for our future.

With a PhD in Geosciences from Stanford University and postdoctoral research at the British Geological Survey, I've developed expertise in sedimentology, stratigraphy, and paleoclimatology. My work has taken me to six continents, from the Arctic tundra to the Australian outback.

Currently, I lead the Terrestrial Evolution Research Group, where we investigate the relationship between geological processes and ecological systems.
